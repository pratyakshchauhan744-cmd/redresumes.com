About RedResumes

RedResumes is a free online resume builder for job seekers who want an ATS-friendly resume without paying for design help or a download fee. The builder walks users through a guided, step-by-step process covering work history, education, and skills, then applies one of several templates built to pass Applicant Tracking Systems used by employers on platforms such as LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor. An AI writing assistant suggests bullet points and a professional summary based on the experience and target role a user enters, which can be edited or accepted as written. Unlike many resume builders that lock the finished PDF behind a paywall, RedResumes exports the completed resume at no cost and without requiring account creation. Most users complete a resume from a blank page to a finished, downloadable PDF in about 15 minutes, with no time limit on edits before exporting.
